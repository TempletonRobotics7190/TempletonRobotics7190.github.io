About Us

We are a club of students from grades 8 to 12 at Templeton Secondary, in East Van. We are enthusiastic learners who are interested in STEM, business and marketing. We participate in the FIRST Robotics Competition.

Our club was founded in Nov. 2017.

The Puddle Jumpers

The Puddle Jumpers are the all-female and gender minority robotics team within the Templeton Robotics Club.

This team was founded in Sept. 2020.
