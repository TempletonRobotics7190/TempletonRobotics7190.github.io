Our Robots

Rythmo - 2024

Rythmo was built by team 7190 for the musically themed 2024 Crescendo FIRST Robotics Competition. Rythmo could pick up and shoot foam rings with great accuracy, positioning 12th out of 36th in qualifications.

Wadlow - 2023

Wadlow was the robot built for FRC Charged Up 2023.

Jammer - 2022

Jammer was the robot built for FRC Rapid React 2022.
